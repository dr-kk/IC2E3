Chairs' Welcome Note


Dear Colleagues,

On behalf of all members of the steering, organizing, and technical program committees, we are pleased to invite you to join us in the fifth edition of the International Conference on Smart Energy Systems and Technologies, which will be held in Eindhoven, The Netherlands, from 5 to 7 September 2022.

The area of smart energy systems and technologies has witnessed both impressive growth and significant investments in recent years. Smart energy systems and technologies enable more efficient management of energy usage and supply and better planning for outage response and recovery, as well as the integration of heterogeneous technologies such as electric vehicle networks, renewable energy systems and connected consumers. Nonetheless, major research questions and engineering challenges remain, particularly in the light of increasing data streams from different parts and components of energy systems and the need to enhance their physical and cyber resilience.

Our aspiration is for SEST 2022 to provide a venue to discuss potential solutions to existing challenges and bring forward new ones. We strive to bring together academic researchers, industry practitioners and regulators so that SEST 2022 constitutes a forum for exchanging knowledge and best practices related to the design and implementation of smart energy systems and technologies.

We are looking forward to receiving your contribution to SEST 2022 and welcoming you to Eindhoven in September 2022.

Nikolaos Paterakis
General Chair of SEST 2022

Miadreza Shafie-khah
General Co-Chair of SEST 2022
